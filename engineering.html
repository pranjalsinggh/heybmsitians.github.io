Choose Your Branch!

1) Computer Science and Engineering(CSE)

This branch focuses on involving several computer science aspects essential for the creation of the computer system.
2) Information Science and Engineering(ISE)

This branch focuses on collecting, categorizing, strategizing, and storage of information.
3) Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

This branch focuses on technology for creating intelligent machines which can think and work like human beings.
4) Electronics and Communication Engineering

Involves researching, designing, developing, and testing of electronic equipment used in various systems.

5) Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering

This branch focuses on designing and overseeing the installation of telecommunications equipment and facilities.
6) Electrical and Electronics Engineering

This branch focuses on a wide range of components, devices and systems, from tiny microchips to huge power station generators, it deals with the technology of electricity.

7) Civil Engineering

This branch focuses on design and maintainance of roads, bridges, dams, and similar structures.
8) Mechanical Engineering

This branch focuses on problem-solving techniques of engineering from design to manufacturing to the marketplace for any object
